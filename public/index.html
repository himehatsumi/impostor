      How to play

      Roles: Everyone gets the same secret word except one Impostor, who gets a related clue instead.

      Clues: Each round has two clue phases. Give a short clue about the word without saying it. The impostor tries to blend in.

      Voting: After clues, everyone votes for who they think is the impostor, or Skip to eliminate no one.

      Winning: Crewmates win by voting out the impostor. The impostor wins by surviving all rounds.
