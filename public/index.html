What is Impostor?

A social deduction word game where one player doesn't know the secret word. Can you find the impostor before they figure it out?

Quick Start

  1. Create a room and share the code with friends
  2. Join using the 4-letter code
  3. Start the game when everyone is ready
  4. Give clues about your word (or fake it!)
  5. Vote for who you think is the impostor

      How to play

      Roles

      Crewmates: Everyone gets the same secret word and must give clues about it.

      Impostor: One player doesn't know the word. They get a related clue instead and must blend in by giving fake clues.

      Gameplay

      Round Structure: Each round has two clue phases where players give short hints about their word (or fake it if you're the impostor).

      Clue Rules: You cannot say the secret word itself or any part of it. Be creative but not too obvious!

      Discussion: After clues are submitted, discuss with other players to figure out who seems suspicious.

      Voting

      After both clue rounds, everyone votes for who they think is the impostor. You can also vote to skip and eliminate no one.

      The player with the most votes is eliminated. If there's a tie or majority skip, no one is eliminated.

      Winning

      Crewmates win by voting out the impostor before the rounds end.

      Impostor wins by surviving all rounds OR by correctly guessing the secret word at any time during the game.
